About Project

This project aims to create a database-driven web application that takes into account all the general information of kinases, Inhibitors and Phosphorylation sites. Additionally, users can also upload phosphoproteomic data to visualize in a graphical representation. The website was developed by a group of five students part of MSc Bioinformatics from the Queen Mary's University of London, under the supervision of Prof Conrad Bessant and Dr Fabrizio Smeraldi.
